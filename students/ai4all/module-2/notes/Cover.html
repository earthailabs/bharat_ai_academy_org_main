How to Use This Book

In Module 1 you learned how computers and AI work. Now you will create — posts, blogs, graphics, and videos — using AI as your team of assistants.

The colored boxes

The same boxes from Module 1 continue here:

🔑 AI Term

A new word you should learn. These words appear again and again in the content-creation world.

💡 Analogy

A difficult idea explained with an everyday example — like comparing an algorithm to a chai stall owner.

📝 Remember This

Short formulas and rules worth memorising — like the Hook formula and the Image Prompt formula.

🎯 Try It Now

A hands-on activity. Stop reading and do it. In this module, almost every section has one — because content creation is learned by creating.

⚠️ Common Mistake

Errors most beginner creators make — so you can avoid them from day one.

What you need for this module

  • A laptop or a smartphone with internet
  • A free ChatGPT or Gemini account (from Module 1)
  • A free Canva account (we will create it in Part 3)
  • A free CapCut account (we will create it in Part 4)
  • This book and a notebook for ideas
