How to Use This Book

Welcome! This book is your companion for Module 1. Read it slowly, try every activity, and keep it with you in every class.

The colored boxes

Whenever you see a colored box, it means something special:

🔑 AI Term

A new word you should learn. These words appear again and again in the AI world.

💡 Analogy

A difficult idea explained with an everyday example — like comparing a computer to a cupboard.

📝 Remember This

Short formulas and rules worth memorising. Perfect for quick revision before class.

🎯 Try It Now

A hands-on activity. Stop reading and do it on your laptop or phone. This is where real learning happens.

⚠️ Common Mistake

Errors most beginners make — so you can avoid them from day one.

Your study routine

  1. Read the part before its class if you can.
  2. In class, follow along and ask questions freely.
  3. After class, complete the Try It Now activities again on your own.
  4. Take the quiz at the end of each part. Answers are at the back.
