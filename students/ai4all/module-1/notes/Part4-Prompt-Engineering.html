Chapter 1

What is a Prompt?

Everything you've learned so far comes together here. The prompt is your steering wheel for AI.

🔑 AI Term — Prompt

A prompt is the instruction you give to an AI. It can be one word or a full page. The quality of the AI's answer depends heavily on the quality of your prompt.

🔑 AI Term — Prompt Engineering

Prompt Engineering is the skill of writing clear, complete prompts that get exactly the answer you want. It is one of the most valuable AI skills — and it needs zero coding.

Weak prompt vs strong prompt — see the difference

❌ Weak Promptwrite about exercise

The AI must guess everything: For whom? How long? What style? You'll get a generic essay.

✅ Strong PromptYou are a friendly fitness coach. Write a simple 7-day home exercise plan for a 19-year-old college student with no gym access. Only 20 minutes per day. Format: a table with Day, Exercise, and Time.

Same AI, completely different result — personal, useful, ready to follow.

💡 Analogy — The Smart New Assistant

Imagine hiring a brilliant assistant on their first day. They are extremely capable but know nothing about you. Say "handle my work" and they'll be lost. Say "reply to this email politely, decline the meeting, suggest Friday instead" — perfection. AI is that assistant. Prompts are your instructions.

The Prompt Formula

Memorise one formula and you can write a strong prompt for anything.

📝 Remember This — THE Formula

ROLE + TASK + CONTEXT + FORMAT

RoleWho should AI be?
+
TaskWhat to do?
+
ContextDetails about you
+
FormatShape of answer

Each piece explained

PieceQuestion it answersExample
RoleWho should the AI pretend to be?"You are an experienced career counsellor."
TaskWhat exactly do you want?"Suggest 3 career paths."
ContextWhat should it know about you / the situation?"I am 18, from Haryana, good at math, family budget is limited."
FormatHow should the answer look?"A table with career, first step, and monthly cost."

The formula in action

Full Formula PromptYou are an experienced career counsellor. ← ROLE Suggest 3 career paths I can start this year. ← TASK I am 18, from Haryana, good at math, just finished 12th, family budget is limited. ← CONTEXT Answer as a table: Career | First Step | Cost. ← FORMAT
🎯 Try It Now

Send the prompt above to ChatGPT — but replace the context with YOUR real details. Read the answer. Then remove the Role and Context lines and send just "suggest career paths". Compare the two answers. Feel the difference the formula makes!

Three Levels of Prompting

Grow from beginner to advanced with three levels. Each level adds one new trick.

Level 1 — The Clear Ask (the formula)

What you learned in Chapter 2: Role + Task + Context + Format. This alone puts you ahead of 90% of AI users.

Level 2 — Show an Example

Want the answer in a specific style? Show the AI an example of what you like. AI is excellent at copying patterns.

Level 2 PromptWrite 3 Instagram captions for a home bakery. Here is an example of the style I like: "Fresh out of the oven, straight into your heart 🍞✨" Match this style: short, warm, one emoji pair.

Level 3 — Ask for Step-by-Step Thinking

For difficult questions — math, decisions, planning — ask the AI to think step by step before answering. This dramatically improves accuracy.

Level 3 PromptI can spend 3 hours daily studying and I have 4 subjects with exams in 20 days. Think step by step, then create the best daily study timetable for me.
📝 Remember This

Level 1: Ask clearly (formula) · Level 2: Show an example · Level 3: Ask for step-by-step thinking. Mix all three for your hardest tasks.

Improving Prompts — The Art of Iteration

Nobody writes the perfect prompt on the first try — and nobody needs to. The secret of professionals is iteration: improving through follow-ups.

🔑 AI Term — Iteration

Iteration means improving something step by step through repeated attempts. With AI: get an answer → give feedback → get a better answer → repeat.

The feedback moves — your follow-up toolkit

If the answer is…Say this
Too long"Make it half the length."
Too difficult"Explain it like I'm 15, with an everyday example."
Too generic"Make it specific to a college student in Haryana."
Wrong tone"Make it more friendly / more formal."
Wrong shape"Give it as a table / bullet points / numbered steps."
Almost right"Keep points 1 and 3, replace point 2 with something about savings."
Not sure it's right"What are the weaknesses of this answer? What did you assume?"
💡 Analogy — The Tailor's Fitting

A tailor never stitches a perfect suit blindly. First fitting → "tighter here, longer there" → second fitting → perfect. Treat every AI answer as a first fitting. Your feedback is the measurement for the next one.

🎯 Try It Now — The 3-Round Challenge

Ask AI: "Write a short message inviting my friend to study together." Then improve it in exactly 3 follow-ups: (1) make it funnier, (2) make it shorter, (3) add a specific time and place. Watch the message get better each round.

Common Prompting Mistakes

Avoid these five mistakes and you're instantly better than most AI users.

⚠️ Mistake 1 — The One-Word Prompt

"resume" ... "marketing" ... "essay". The AI must guess everything. Fix: use the formula — Role + Task + Context + Format.

⚠️ Mistake 2 — No Context About You

Asking "suggest a business idea" without saying your budget, city, skills, or interests. Fix: two lines of context change everything.

⚠️ Mistake 3 — Accepting the First Answer

The first answer is a first draft. Fix: always improve it with at least one follow-up (Chapter 4 toolkit).

⚠️ Mistake 4 — Asking Two Things in One Breath

"Write my resume and also plan my diet and also explain AI" — messy answers. Fix: one task per message. New topic → new chat.

⚠️ Mistake 5 — Blind Trust

Submitting AI facts without checking (remember hallucinations!). Fix: verify anything important — dates, numbers, names, medical or legal claims.

📝 Remember This

Be specific · Give context · Iterate · One task at a time · Verify.

Chapter 7 · Mini Project

Build Your Personal AI Assistant

Time to combine everything: the formula, context, iteration — into one project you'll keep using after class.

Step 1 — Write your assistant prompt

  • Open ChatGPT or Gemini and start a new chat. Name it "My AI Assistant".
  • Copy the template below and fill in your details.
  • Send it. Your assistant is born!
Your Assistant TemplateYou are my personal assistant and coach. ← ROLE Help me with studies, health, and daily planning. ← TASK About me: I am [age] years old, living in [city]. I am currently [studying/working on ...]. My main goal this month is [goal]. My daily schedule: [brief schedule]. ← CONTEXT Always reply in simple English, keep answers short, and end with one clear next step. ← FORMAT

Step 2 — Test it with real questions

  • "Plan my study schedule for tomorrow."
  • "Suggest a healthy breakfast I can make in 10 minutes."
  • "I'm feeling unmotivated — help me start."
  • "What should I focus on this week for my goal?"

Step 3 — Improve it (iteration!)

Answers too long? Too generic? Use your Chapter 4 feedback moves. Add more context. Adjust the personality. After 3–4 rounds of improvement, your assistant will feel truly yours.

📝 Remember This

Keep your assistant in one chat and return to it daily (the One-Chat Rule). In the next module, you'll turn this prompt into a reusable Skill file in your AI Workspace!
